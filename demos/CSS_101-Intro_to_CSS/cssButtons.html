Sliding Doors technique

Many new pages are populated with a pluthera of images for buttons generated for sake of consistency. However even in this pattern of images many of the images do not match exactly in color - or even the effect of a click as a button.

Since we want our buttons to be uber-flexible, we'll have to make the background image expand with the size of the button's text. For that, we'll use the beloved Sliding Doors; two complementing images creating the illusion of a single, stretching image.

Our button will be a basic <a> tag with a nested  <span> , each containing a different slice of the background image.
