It is a period of techno unrest... Rebel Browser makers, Dogs and Cats playing together, Mass histeria... It is good against evil lurking in the darkness. The rebels have won their first victory against the evil Galactic Windows Empire.

During the battle, Rebel browser makers managed to deploy secret plans to unveil better web pages through HTML5. The Empire’s ultimate weapon, the INTERNET EXPLODER, a security plagued, feature lacking internet browser threatens to destroy an entire planet.

Pursued by the Empire’s sinister IE user agents, Firefox and Webkit browsers manage to stay leaps and bounds beyond the reach of IE. In their quest to be the best custodians of HTML 5 agents that can save our people and restore freedom to the galaxy... Yoda Yoda Yoda....
