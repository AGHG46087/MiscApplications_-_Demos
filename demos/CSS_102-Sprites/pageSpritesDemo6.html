Sliding Doors technique buttons with CSS

How many images do you see on this page?
One Two Three Four Five or more

Many new pages are populated with a pluthera of images for buttons generated for sake of consistency. However even in this pattern of creative agency image buttons, many of the images do not match exactly in color - or even the visual effect of a click as a button.

Since we want our buttons to be uber-flexible, we'll have to make the background image expand with the size of the button's text. For that, we'll use Sliding Doors two complementing images creating the illusion of a single, stretching image.

Our button will be a basic <a> tag with a nested  <span> , each containing a different slice of the background image. Even this technique has its limitations when buttons are needed to be really large. As you can see the button breaks down.
