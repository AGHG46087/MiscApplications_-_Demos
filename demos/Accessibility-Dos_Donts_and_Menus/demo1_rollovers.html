Demo: Rollover Images

Place your mouse over the following image to see a JavaScript example that does not impact accessibility.

Problems

None. In this example, there is no important content or functionality introduced by the JavaScript. The swapping of images is purely cosmetic.

Solution

Progressive enhancement of a section of a page and adding JavaScript to it. The JavaScript is non-instrusive. No additional accessibility techniques are required. Remember, the image itself must have alternative text (i.e., <img alt="home" />).
